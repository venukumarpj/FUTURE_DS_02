# FUTURE_DS_02

📊 Customer Churn & Retention Analysis
Task 2 — Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship
Diagnosing why customers leave, quantifying the revenue at risk, and building a data-driven retention strategy — end to end.

Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of customer retention and churn behavior using the Telco Customer Churn dataset. The dashboard helps identify factors contributing to customer churn and enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions to improve customer retention and reduce revenue loss.

Objectives: Monitor overall customer retention and churn performance. Identify high-risk customer segments. Analyze the impact of contract types, payment methods, internet services, and tenure on customer churn. Support strategic decision-making through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

Dashboard Features: Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Total Customers Churn Rate (%) Retention Rate (%)

Interactive Visualizations Churn by Contract Type

Analyzes customer churn across:

Month-to-Month One-Year Two-Year Contracts Tenure Analysis

Segments customers into tenure groups: 0–12 Months 12–24 Months 24–48 Months 48+ Months Helps identify when customers are most likely to churn.

Internet Service Analysis: Compares churn behavior across different internet service categories.

Payment Method Analysis: Evaluates the relationship between payment methods and customer retention.

Churn by Tenure & Charges Scatter plot visualizing: Customer Tenure Monthly Charges Churn Status Used to identify customer risk patterns and spending behaviors.

The dashboard includes dynamic slicers for: Contract Type Business Insights

This dashboard helps answer key business questions such as:

Which contract type experiences the highest churn? Do customers with higher monthly charges churn more frequently? At what stage of the customer lifecycle is churn most common? Which payment methods are associated with higher customer retention? What customer segments should be prioritized for retention campaigns?

Tools & Technologies Microsoft Power BI Power Query DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) Data Modeling Interactive Data Visualization Dataset

Dataset used: Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographics, account information, service subscriptions, billing details, and churn status
